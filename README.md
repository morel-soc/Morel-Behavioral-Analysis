# Behavioral Analysis & Case Studies

This repository contains structured case analyses focused on behavioral patterns, decision-making, and incident flow.

The work here examines how real-world situations unfold; from initial interaction through escalation, investigation, and resolution.

---

## Approach

Each case is analyzed with attention to:

- behavioral patterns  
- escalation triggers  
- decision-making under pressure  
- law enforcement response and procedure  
- overall case trajectory  

The focus is not on the event itself, but on how and why it developed.

---

## Scope

Cases included may involve:

- medical or cognitive impairment incidents  
- social engineering and scam behavior  
- misidentification or perception-based escalation  
- interactions between individuals and systems (law enforcement, care, or response)

---

## Purpose

This work is intended to build a deeper understanding of:

- how early signals influence outcomes  
- how behavior shifts under stress or confusion  
- how systems respond to complex, real-world scenarios
